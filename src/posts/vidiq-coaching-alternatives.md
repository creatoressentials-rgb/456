VidIQ's coaching program is well-known, but it's far from your only option. If you're looking for personalized YouTube coaching, here's a clear breakdown of what VidIQ offers — and seven alternatives worth considering.

## VidIQ's YouTube Coaching Program

VidIQ's coaching targets creators who want structured, data-driven guidance with emphasis on channel analytics, SEO, and content strategy.

**What you get:**
- Dedicated 1-on-1 Creator Coach for audits and strategy
- Channel analysis to identify improvement areas
- SEO-focused content planning (titles, thumbnails, keywords)

**Price:** $199/month ($99/month paid annually)

## 7 Alternatives to VidIQ Coaching

### 1. Creator Essentials

**Price:** $89/month — 7-day free trial + 30-day money-back guarantee

Creator Essentials coaching focuses on giving creators clear, actionable guidance without confusion and endless trial and error. The program is built around channels that attract the right audience, maintain engagement, and generate income.

**What you get:**
- Coaching directly from the founder — not a rotating team of coaches
- Comprehensive channel audit covering content, thumbnails, SEO, and engagement
- Monthly reports and competitor benchmarking
- Affordable pricing designed for creators at any stage

### 2. LaShonda Brown

**Price:** $500 for a 60-minute session / $50/month membership  
**Specializes in:** SEO-driven growth and lead generation for service-based businesses

### 3. Christine Kaaloa

**Price:** $250 for a 60-minute session  
**Specializes in:** Travel vlogging and niche content strategy, plus mindset coaching for creators overcoming fear of camera

### 4. Creator Coach

**Price:** Custom pricing for 45-minute sessions  
**Specializes in:** Work-life balance, stress management, and sustainable long-term creator success. Works with channels ranging from new to 500K+ subscribers

### 5. Acceleratus Media (Owen Video)

**Price:** Custom pricing  
**Specializes in:** YouTube for businesses and executives. Lead-generation strategies, VA training, and done-for-you channel optimization for brands using YouTube as a primary marketing channel

### 6. Erika Vieira

**Price:** 5-figure investment  
**Specializes in:** Women YouTubers targeting growth from 100K to 1M+ subscribers. Deep-dive branding, audience psychology, and daily channel monitoring

### 7. Creator People

**Price:** $1,100 for 3–4 sessions (1.5 hours each)  
**Specializes in:** Multi-session structured coaching with in-depth analytics and SEO training

## Which Is Right for You?

The right coaching program depends on your goals, budget, and where your channel currently stands.

If you're looking for affordable, personalized, expert coaching with a risk-free trial — [Creator Essentials](/services/youtube-coaching-program/) is designed specifically for that.

If you're a business owner using YouTube for leads, Owen Video's focus on ROI and conversion may be a better fit.

If you're a solo creator ready for a premium, high-investment engagement, Erika Vieira works at a different scale.

The most important thing: don't keep guessing on your own when expert guidance is available at multiple price points.
