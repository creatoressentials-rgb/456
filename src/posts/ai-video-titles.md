Standing out among millions of YouTube videos is challenging. AI tools — specifically ChatGPT — offer a practical way to generate title ideas faster and with more strategic grounding than starting from a blank page.

Here's a simple, effective approach.

## The AI Title Generation Strategy

The core idea is using top-ranking videos in your niche as input — giving the AI real data about what's already performing well, then generating variations you can make your own.

### The Prompt

Use this as a starting point:

> "I need to write a video title for my new YouTube video about [your topic]. Here are some top-ranking videos about this topic. Please provide video title ideas based on these titles: [Title 1] [Title 2] [Title 3]"

To find the top-ranking titles, simply search your topic on YouTube and note the titles of the top results. These are the videos the algorithm has validated — understanding their structure is valuable.

## How to Use AI Output Responsibly

AI gives you a starting point, not a finished product. The goal is inspiration and speed — not imitation.

**Do:**
- Use AI suggestions as raw material and reshape them in your own voice
- Identify the pattern or structure that makes the top titles work, then apply that to your angle
- Ask the AI to generate multiple variations — pick the strongest elements from several

**Don't:**
- Publish an AI-generated title without reviewing and personalizing it
- Use a title that copies a competitor's phrasing too closely
- Let the AI replace your judgment about what's true to your content and brand

## Refining Your Prompt

You can get more specific results by telling the AI what tone or angle you want:

- "Make the title more urgent"
- "Make it funnier while keeping it informative"
- "Make it sound like it's aimed at beginners"
- "Focus the title on the outcome, not the process"

Small adjustments to your prompt produce meaningfully different results. Experiment until the suggestions feel closer to what you'd actually write.

## AI as a Creative Catalyst

The best use of AI in your title workflow isn't replacement — it's momentum. When you're staring at a blank field wondering what to call a video, having 10 AI-generated options to react to is almost always faster than generating your first idea from scratch.

React to the options, combine what you like, and write the final version yourself. That's the workflow.
